Private & Government Sponsored Accelerators

Does your accelerator need training workshops for entrepreneurs?

  • We provide interactive workshops from 1 or 2 hours or half day, and
  • 2 to 7-day programs, depending on your needs and budget.

We’ve delivered workshops around the world for 1000’s of entrepreneurs. Our workshops are skills based. We demonstrate a point – then have the attendees work on their solution and present the result. The participants engage and present their work actively throughout the day.

We know from experience that many entrepreneurs will avoid standing up and participating. We provide 1-on-1 coaching in presenting and have everyone get up and present several times during the program. The result is a clear increase in confidence!

These are workshops specifically geared for go-to-market activities to engage customers. We stress customer focused, build the sales pipeline, use metrics, budgeting and cash management and proper governance to achieve results.

The emphasis is on participants presenting key points and engaging the other attendees to make consensus. The goal is that they learn both leadership and presentation skills.

We prefer to work with startups that all ready have a product concept and are ready to go-to-market. We are experienced in software, hardware, IoT and B2B sales and operations.

The output is a complete pitch deck and practice in delivery and body language.
